About Me

Kel Schmutz writes YA Fantasy and speculative fiction with a touch of romance. Influenced by a lifelong love of classic fairy tales and folklore, she is particularly interested in magic as a metaphor for reality. She loves reading horror novels, romance manhwa, and fairy tale anthologies, and is always on the hunt for stories that feature the strange or inexplicable.

Kel is a developmental editor with Sit Write Here's Path to Publication. Her story A Charmed Life was selected as a finalist for the WOW! Women on Writing Spring 2024 flash fiction contest. You can read her interview with WOW! Women on Writing here.

To find out more about Kel's upcoming novel, sign up for her newsletter. You can also connect with her on Instagram at KelSchmutz, or reach her via email at kel@kelschmutz.com
