About Me

Kel Schmutz writes speculative fiction and fantasy, always with a touch of romance. Hugely influenced by her love of folklore and mythology, she is particularly interested in magic as a metaphor for reality. She reads everything from children's literature to horror to classic fantasy to japanese manga, and is always on the hunt for books that feature the strange or inexplicable or wonderful.

Kel is a developmental editor with Sit Write Here's Path to Publication. Her story A Charmed Life was selected as a finalist for the WOW! Women on Writing Spring 2024 flash fiction contest. You can read it here, and read her interview with WOW! Women on Writing here.

To find out more about Kel's books and short stories, sign up for her newsletter at the top of the page. She sends out one email at the end of each month with recommendations, book breakdowns from a writer's perspective, and sneak peeks at what she's working on.

You can connect with her on Instagram at Kel.Schmutz, or reach her via email at kel@kelschmutz.com
